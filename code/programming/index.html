Programming is a Big Field.


Programming is becoming more and more useful to our society every day.

Things need to become more automated. More tasks need to be accomplished. Programming jobs are in high demand.

Being able to program and even just understand the basics of something like Computer Science is a highly valued skill in today's marketplace.

What Are Its Applications?

Programming has expanded into such a wide range of things that it's hard to point down exactly what it can be used for. Chief among what it can be used for, though, are:

  • Robotics
  • Computer Scripting
  • Games
  • Web Development
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Data Science
  • Software Building
  • Systems Engineering
  • IT Tasks

The Importance of Programming


This all goes back into the reason I am making this site. I want to help others learn about Computer Science and its uses.

Programming & Computation


Programming, in a sense, has been around for a long time. The idea of giving machines specific instructions didn't just magically pop up in the 1980s.

With computers starting to pop up after World War II, the people who worked to crack codes and perform complex tasks for the Allies started to develop important ideas.

The ideas they came up with would later be the basis for all of modern Computer Science.

For all of its importance today, it hasn't actually been around all that long.

But don't let that fool you. There are plenty of people who are extremely knowledgable about the field.

You already know what Computer Science is now and what it has been.

The question is: What will it be in the future? Will you have an impact on that future?
